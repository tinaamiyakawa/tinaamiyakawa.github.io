The Rodina

Thursday, January 20th 2017

The Rodina was founded in 2011 by Tereza and Vit Ruller. Czech-born, Amsterdam based independent graphic designers. The Rodina works on commissioned design projects, while also experimenting autonomously. The Rodina invents the ways how experience, knowledge and relations are preserved and produced. Interested in connections between culture, technology and aesthetic, The Rodina designs events, objects and tools.
