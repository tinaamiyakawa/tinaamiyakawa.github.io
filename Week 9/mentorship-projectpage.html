This project is a collection of explorations, notes and thoughts created over the course of 16 weeks between January and May of 2016. It started with a series of obstructions and a desire to establish my genuine interests, while pushing the boundaries of pre-existing formal skills. I feel that this is a reflection of my current state of mind, and consider this final publication to be the beginning of something greater, not the end.

Mentored by Chris Svensson.
